About The Festival

India's Most Celebrated Cultural Fest

Utsav Events brings together the very best of India's music, culture, and entertainment under one grand stage. From the bustling streets of Hyderabad to the vibrant shores of Mumbai and the garden city of Bengaluru, we celebrate the soul of India.

Our events attract over 50,000 attendees each year, featuring 100+ artists from across different genres and regional music traditions — Tollywood, Bollywood, Kollywood, Carnatic, Hindustani, Folk, and more.
